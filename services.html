OUR SERVICES



WE OFFER A VARIETY OF THERMAL MANAGEMENT SERVICES



At Thermistance, we provide a variety of thermal engineering services to help bring your ideas to life. Our services range from product design to high-volume production of thermal management solutions, and everything in between. Our team of experienced engineers and technology developers ensures that we can offer a wide range of services with high-quality results. We also pride ourselves on our efficient program management structure, which allows us to offer competitive prices while maintaining strict confidentiality to protect our customers' valuable information.



THERMAL MANAGEMENT CONSULTING

Thermistance is a team of thermal engineering experts who can assist clients in various industries like military, telecommunications, medical equipment, manufacturing, and aerospace. They can suggest efficient and affordable thermal solutions by studying the feasibility of potential solutions in terms of cost, performance, reliability, and manufacturability. This helps clients save time and money by avoiding delays at the end of a project. Thermistance can also compare multiple potential solutions to determine the best option for performance and value.



DESIGN AND ANALYSIS

Thermistance has a team of thermal engineers who are skilled in analyzing heat transfer and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD). They are proficient in using popular software like SolidWorks, MATLAB, CATIA, and ANSYS for CAD modeling. Moreover, they can also create and modify computational codes as per project requirements. The thermal engineering experts at Thermistance have experience in simulating thermal phenomena at different scales, from small to large.
