About Us

Being a start-up, Thermistance Technologies is an Indian pioneer with advanced cooling technology as their expertise. Our main focus is on developing a miniaturized Loop Heat Pipe technology for varied applications, including gaming, high-performance computing, and much more. We are the only company in the market to commercialize and make this state of the art technology available at minimum cost.

Our Mission

Become a trusted partner in the world’s technological revolution by removing barriers to efficient thermal management.

Our Values

Maintaining our integrity while being ethical and giving our excellent performance backed up by the pillars of teamwork.

Our Vision

Develop cost effective advanced passive technology for our customers, so they can design their products without thermal limitations and advance the technology for the benefits of humanity.

Find Out More About Us

Thermistance was created by the passionate IIT alumni with the aim of providing world class facilities and research hub for thermal management problems. We are working round the clock to provide superior thermal management solutions.

Our research and development team is working on the commercialization of miniature Loop Heat Pipe technology for electronic cooling applications. We also provide conventional heat pipes, thermosyphons, heat exchangers, heat pipes with jackets, pin-fins, thermal storage, customized porous structures for battery, and water purification applications.

Our expert team also provides consultancy services for advanced research avenues from problem defining to the end-use solution provider.
